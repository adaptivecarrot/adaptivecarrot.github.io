# Interesting words
> Just a bunch of words that are curious to me.

### Opposites
- **Egregious**: used to mean the opposite of what it does now.
- **Literally**: usually means figuratively. Possibly taking the path "egregious" took?
- **Sanction**: "the law was sanctioned" vs "the nation was sanctioned"
