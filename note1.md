What makes the world run? It is not 5-year plans or _ as secondary school might have impressed upon you. Much of it is organic, like neurons forming connections to other neurons, or like a sapling growing into a tree ___. 
The exercise of understanding the world is not prescriptive: we are not elaborating on how humanity has created what we now enjoy; 
it is descriptive: the world has gone through a certain path through all the possible paths, to attain the path in which we find ourselves in; what is the state of this world, and how did we get here?
